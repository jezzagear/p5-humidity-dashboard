Tub air-pressure analysis

With sealed bungs and rigid PC tubs, the trapped air in each tub is a closed system. PV = nRT with constant V and n means the pressure swings with temperature. This page shows tub pressure differential (ΔP vs atmospheric) over the 6-year ERA5 weather period. Sealed at 23 °C / 50 % RH / 101.325 kPa.
